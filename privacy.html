3. How We Use Your Data

  • Processing and fulfilling your orders.
  • Sending order confirmations and shipping updates.
  • Responding to customer support queries.
  • Improving our website and product offerings.
  • Sending promotional emails (only with your consent — unsubscribe anytime).
  • Complying with legal and regulatory obligations.

4. Payment Data & Razorpay

RazorPay
Secure Payments via Razorpay
All payment transactions are processed by Razorpay Software Pvt. Ltd., a PCI-DSS Level 1 certified payment gateway. Jersify never stores, sees, or has access to your card number, CVV, or banking credentials. Payment data is collected and encrypted directly by Razorpay. Refer to Razorpay's Privacy Policy at razorpay.com/privacy.

We receive only a transaction confirmation and masked payment reference from Razorpay to complete your order.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only with trusted partners necessary for operations:

  • Razorpay — payment processing.
  • Shipping / courier partners — name, address, phone for delivery.
  • Analytics tools — anonymised usage data only.
  • Legal authorities — only when required by law.

6. Cookies

We use cookies to keep your cart active, remember your preferences, and analyse site traffic. Types we use:

  • Essential cookies — required for the site to function (cart, session).
  • Analytics cookies — help us understand how visitors use the site.
  • Marketing cookies — used to show relevant promotions (optional).

You can disable non-essential cookies in your browser settings at any time.

7. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored on secure servers. We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your data against unauthorised access, loss, or misuse.

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services, comply with legal obligations, and resolve disputes. You may request deletion at any time.

8. Your Rights

Under applicable Indian data protection laws, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten").
  • Opt out of marketing communications at any time.
  • Withdraw consent where processing is based on consent.

To exercise any of these rights, email us at support@jersify.online.

9. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
